03 — Methodology

A delivery model that protects momentum.

Fixed-scope sprints, senior-led teams, and a single definition of done: the metric moved.

  1. 01

    Audit & North Star

    Two weeks of deep discovery: revenue model, ICP, analytics, tech stack, competitive field. We leave with a prioritized roadmap tied to a single north-star metric.

    • Revenue & CAC teardown
    • Technical & SEO audit
    • Positioning workshop
  2. 02

    Design & Systems

    We design the brand surface, information architecture and component system — then codify it into a living design system your team can extend for years.

    • Brand & art direction
    • Design system in Figma
    • Prototype & user testing
  3. 03

    Build & Launch

    Senior engineers ship in two-week increments on Next.js, Astro or headless stacks. QA, accessibility and performance budgets are enforced in CI — not after launch.

    • Next.js / Astro / headless
    • CI/CD & observability
    • Launch war room
  4. 04

    Compound & Operate

    Post-launch we operate the growth engine: SEO, CRO, lifecycle, and AI workflows. We review outcomes with your leadership monthly and publish a quarterly impact report.

    • Monthly growth sprints
    • Attribution & reporting
    • Quarterly executive review
